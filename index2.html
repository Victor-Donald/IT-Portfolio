Project 3: Enterprise Hardware Deployment & OS Specialization

Timeline: June 2025 – Present

🛠️ Technical Execution

Architected a standardized deployment framework to transition a diverse hardware fleet from generic Windows 11 environments to optimized local configurations. Successfully deployed tailored Surface Recovery Images for specialized endpoints, including Surface Pro 7+ and Surface Go 2 tablets, as well as enterprise-grade HP and Lenovo desktop systems.

⚙️ Driver Injection & OS Modification

Built and deployed custom bootable media using advanced command-line and imaging tools. Dynamically injected device-specific hardware drivers into master deployment files, ensuring 100% out-of-the-box compatibility for peripheral chipsets, network interface cards (NICs), and integrated touch sensors.

💾 Storage Provisioning & Media Management

Engineered high-capacity storage deployment media, implementing file-system formatting protocols across 2TB Western Digital My Passport and 1TB Buffalo external drives. Configured fully optimized, secure bootable drives to support offline local user setups and rapid system recovery.

📈 Business Value & Outcome

Eliminated dependency on active internet connections during deployment, reduced terminal provisioning times, and established a scalable, cross-platform disaster recovery baseline across multiple hardware architectures.

Technical Projects

PC Hardware Optimization & Storage Upgrade

  • Performed physical hardware maintenance and internal component upgrades on mobile computing devices, including safely unseating and replacing internal solid-state drives (SSDs).
  • Managed disk partitioning, drive alignment verification, and storage optimization to ensure system stability and peak data performance.
  • Utilized specialized precision toolkits to safely disassemble, repair, and reassemble complex electronic devices without damaging internal chassis components.

Operating System Deployment & Firmware Recovery

  • Executed clean operating system reinstalls and configured custom local user accounts on refurbished tablet devices.
  • Diagnosed and resolved system driver conflicts, manually deploying and updating firmware to ensure hardware compatibility with peripheral devices like Bluetooth keyboards.
  • Troubleshot seized hardware and internal cooling systems, managing motherboard and heatsink assemblies to resolve thermal throttling and hardware faults.
