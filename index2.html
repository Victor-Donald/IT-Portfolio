Project 3: Enterprise Hardware Deployment & OS Specialization

Timeline: June 2025 – Present

🛠️ Technical Execution

Architected a standardized deployment framework to transition a diverse hardware fleet from generic Windows 11 environments to optimized local configurations.

Successfully deployed tailored Surface Recovery Images for specialized endpoints, including Surface Pro 7+, Surface Go 2 tablets, and enterprise-grade HP and Lenovo desktop systems.

⚙️ Driver Injection & OS Modification

Built and deployed custom bootable media using advanced command-line and imaging tools. Dynamically injected device-specific hardware drivers into master deployment files, ensuring 100% out-of-the-box compatibility for peripheral chipsets, NICs, and integrated touch sensors.

💾 Storage Provisioning & Media Management

Engineered high-capacity storage deployment media, implementing file-system formatting protocols across 2TB Western Digital My Passport and 1TB Buffalo external drives.

Configured secure, optimized bootable drives to support offline local user setups and rapid system recovery.

📈 Business Value & Outcome

Eliminated dependency on active internet connections during deployment, reduced provisioning times, and established a scalable, cross-platform disaster recovery baseline across multiple hardware architectures.
